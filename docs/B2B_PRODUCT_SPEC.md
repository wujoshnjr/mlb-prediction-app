# B2B Product Specification

## Product Positioning

The long-term product is a Sports Intelligence SaaS / B2B API platform.

The product should sell verifiable sports intelligence infrastructure, not guaranteed picks.

## Current Product

Current status:

- Paper-trading research dashboard
- Market comparison evidence pipeline
- CLV tracking
- Model governance reports
- Data quality reports
- Sample state tracking
- Paper-only dashboard

## Target Customers

### B2C Pro / Semi-Pro Users

Potential users:

- professional or semi-professional sports analysts
- research-driven bettors
- DFS / fantasy users
- content creators

Needs:

- dashboard
- market movement
- CLV tracking
- model vs market comparison
- data quality scores

### B2B Media / Content Companies

Potential customers:

- sports media sites
- YouTube / podcast teams
- fantasy sports communities
- sports content platforms

Needs:

- read-only API
- embeddable widgets
- daily summaries
- postgame performance reports

### B2B Trading / Analytics Desks

Potential customers:

- analytics companies
- odds comparison platforms
- sportsbook risk teams
- quant research teams

Needs:

- stable API
- historical data
- model registry
- audit trail
- data quality scores
- CLV reports
- calibration reports
- SLA

- real_money_betting
- automated_wagering
- sportsbook_execution

- commercial_api_distribution_until_license_review

## Commercial Readiness Requirements

Before commercial release:

- legal review
- data license review
- security review
- API key auth
- rate limits
- usage tracking
- billing
- privacy policy
- terms of service
- risk disclosure
- no automated wagering policy
- incident response plan

## Summary

The mature product is not a betting bot.

It is a verifiable sports intelligence infrastructure platform for prediction evidence, market intelligence, model governance, and data quality.
