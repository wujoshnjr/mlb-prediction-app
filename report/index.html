Roadmap

資料源修補優先順序

先修資料，不急著升級模型。
starting_pitcher

Starting pitcher quality, pitch traits, strikeout/walk profile and matchup context are high-leverage MLB signals.

  • pitch_movement_diff
  • pitch_type_matchup_score
  • pitcher_rating_diff
  • sp_csw_diff
  • sp_fip_diff
bullpen

Bullpen availability and workload affect late-game win probability, especially after starter exits.

  • bullpen_ip_diff
  • bullpen_availability_diff
market

Market no-vig probability and odds movement are essential baselines; model promotion should not occur without beating them.

  • odds_change
injuries

Injury context is important but must be carefully timestamped to avoid stale or post-game leakage.

  • injury_diff
lineup

Confirmed lineup and top-order hitter context affect actual game strength and should be tracked before model promotion.

  • platoon_ops_diff
  • top3_woba_diff
  • lineup_context_available
  • catcher_era_diff
  • cs_diff
statcast_batting

Statcast batting quality measures contact quality and batter skill signals that are closer to process than final score outcomes.

  • swing_miss_diff
  • avg_bat_speed_diff
  • barrel_pa_diff
  • hardhit_pa_diff
  • statcast_woba_diff
